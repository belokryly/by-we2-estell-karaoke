Our Story

Where every night
becomes a melody

At Estelle, modern elegance meets lively entertainment. Our sleek and stylish lounge is the ideal setting to unwind with friends, savor handcrafted cocktails and indulge in plates from our diverse kitchen. Our bartenders are masters at crafting both classic and inventive drinks that elevate the night.

Whether you're celebrating a special occasion, enjoying a night out or simply ready to have a blast — Estelle has something for everyone. With a vibrant, friendly atmosphere, it's the perfect place to make memories that last a lifetime.
